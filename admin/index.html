How Pricing Works

Prices saved here are stored in Firebase Firestore and automatically reflected on your main website in real-time. When a customer visits your site, it loads prices from Firestore. The payment amounts on both Cashfree and Razorpay orders are also validated server-side against these stored values.

INR prices are shown to Indian visitors. USD prices are shown to international visitors.
