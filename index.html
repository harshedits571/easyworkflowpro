Edit your generated captions directly in the panel. Changes are instantly reflected on After Effects text layers — no re-generation needed.

  • 📝
    INLINE TEXT EDITING Click any caption to edit its text — changes apply to the AE text layer in real-time
  • 🎯
    LIVE PREVIEW See your edits reflected immediately on the After Effects timeline — zero delay

About Easy Workflow

Easy Workflow Pro is a professional After Effects extension and ScriptUI panel designed for motion designers, video editors, and animators. It provides a complete After Effects workflow toolkit with tools for layer alignment, anchor point adjustment, font replacement, shape layer control, effects management, composition saving, and layer creation — all accessible from a single docked panel.

Compatible with Adobe After Effects 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026 on both Windows and macOS. Easy Workflow is trusted by motion graphics artists who want to speed up repetitive tasks, reduce clicks, and build a faster creative workflow inside After Effects.

Whether you're looking for the best After Effects plugin for alignment, a font replacer tool for AE, a KBar alternative, or a Motion Bro alternative — Easy Workflow Pro offers all of this and more in one affordable, one-time-payment package. Available for download at easyworkflow.store.
